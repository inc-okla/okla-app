Superior Features

Okla Lite is packed with powerful features to supercharge your MidJourney workflow

Auto Prompt Sending

Automatically send thousands of prompts to MidJourney without manual intervention. Queue up your prompts and let Okla Lite handle the rest.

Auto Image Download

Automatically downloads all generated images to your specified folder. Never lose track of your creations again.

Prompt Tracking

Human Verification
